What is FRC?

The FIRST Foundation was established in 1989 to increase young people's interest and participation in science and technology. FIRST; organizes innovative programs that motivate young people to seek education/career opportunities in science, technology, engineering and mathematics while improving their self-confidence, knowledge and life skills.
FRC is an international robot tournament for high school students aged 15-18 each year and has been held annually since 1992. More than 350,000 students from all over the world participate in FRC, which is known as the most prestigious robotics tournament for high school age. Each year, teams try to build robots that can handle tasks set within the framework of a theme announced at the beginning of January.
The program aims to provide students with the real engineering experience, while providing a collaborative working platform with teachers and engineers.
In the FIRST Robotics Competition, which is prepared with the slogan "More than Robot", there should be students who can work in a team in different fields. Teams can find the opportunity to work like a professional business by forming teams such as sponsorship, design, social media management, electronics, programming and mechanics during the preparation processes. This program enables children who want to specialize in different fields to come together and work together as a team within the specified time.
